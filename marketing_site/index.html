How It Works

What SCOUT Does

Three intelligent steps — from raw requirements to a complete, evidence-backed FSD.

01
Ingest
Upload Requirements

Upload a Word or PDF requirements doc, or paste text directly. SCOUT also ingests Confluence baselines, SFCC docs, and historical FSDs as your persistent knowledge base.

02
Analyse
Gap Analysis

Every requirement is classified: OOTB Match, Partial Match, Custom Dev, or Open Question — with a confidence score, source citation, and implementation mode.

03
Generate
FSD Output

A complete FSD is generated — fully editable before publishing. Export as DOCX or publish directly to Confluence with duplicate-title checking built in.

Result: A complete, evidence-backed FSD in under 60 seconds — not 2–5 days
